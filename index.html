Master Industrial

Thesis: Automation of attaching/ detaching of a combine header
The combine vehicle was programmed in Matlab to drive autonomously towards it's target. All aspects of autonomous vehicles (path-planning, path-following, Kalman-Filter,..) were implemented or researched in this thesis. A CAN-Bus system was used to read \& write the actuators. As sensor for detecting the target, a 3D-camera was utilized. This project was in collaboration with CNH Industrial.
